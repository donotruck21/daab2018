Getting There & Accomodations

The venue is in Snohomish and our wedding is on a Friday. Given that our ceremony begins at 5pm, we strongly advise you take at least a half-day off of work that day. Google Maps estimates a little over an hour from Bellevue and a little under 2 hours from Federal Way during high traffic hours. We know it's a little deep, but we promise it will be worth it!
